SUNA BESHA

The Suna Besha festival is observed five times a year with the most importan festival being celebrated on Asadha Ekadashi where the deities are observed in front of the temple on Ratna Singhasana or the chariot.During this festival,the deities are decorated with more than 100 kg of golds.

Nagarjuna Besha

The Nagarjuna Besha is observed during kartika month.Specially on the fifth day of panchuka.It honors Arjuna's conflict with his son Nagarjuna or Sage Sahasrarjun's demise at the hands of Sage Parsuram. Deities are decked with golden handa and other gold-based weaponry during this time.

GAHANANA BESHA

On Snana Purnima tithi(the full day in Jyestha),the bathing ceremony of the deities is observed on the Sanavedi .Thereafter,the deities are dressed like Ganesa who bears the head of an elephant(Hati).Lord Jagannath and Balabhadra appear like Ganesa or black and white elephants
